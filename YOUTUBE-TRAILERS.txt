The preview thumbnails are loaded from YouTube, and the privacy-enhanced YouTube player
is created only after the visitor clicks a preview. Closing the modal removes the iframe,
which immediately stops video and audio.

To publish, replace your existing index.html, assets/css/styles.css and assets/js/main.js.
The old assets/videos folder can be deleted.
